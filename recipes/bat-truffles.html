Ingredients

  • 1 13"-x-9" pan fudgy brownies, baked and cooked
  • 1/2 c. Chocolate frosting
  • 7 Oreo cookies, halved and stuffing removed
  • 11 oz. semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 c. Black sanding sugar
  • 28 royal icing eyes or red mini M&Ms

Directions

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside. In a large bowl, roughly crumble brownie by hand. Mix in chocolate frosting and gently knead mixture until evenly moistened.
  2. Using a cookie scoop, portion out 1 tablespoon brownie frosting mixture and roll into small balls. Insert two halved Oreo cookies on either side to create wings. Place brownie balls on the prepared baking sheet and freeze until firm, 2 hours.
  3. Remove balls from freezer and let come to room temperature, 15 minutes
  4. Melt chocolate in a microwave-safe bowl in 5-second intervals. Dip bat brownie truffles in chocolate and dust with black sanding sugar. Decorate with royal icing eyes or M&Ms.
